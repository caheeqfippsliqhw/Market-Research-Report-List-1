<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Nonabsorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical surgical sutures market is segmented into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable sutures are designed to break down and be absorbed by the body over time, eliminating the need for removal, making them ideal for internal procedures. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, maintain their integrity and provide long-term support, often used in external wounds or areas needing prolonged stabilization. Both types are essential in various surgical applications, influencing patient outcomes and recovery.</p></p>
